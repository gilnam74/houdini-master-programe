About Me

The heart
behind the art

Hello! I'm Wee, a Canada-based graphic designer with a love for soft aesthetics, meaningful typography, and design that feels both intimate and impactful.

My work lives at the intersection of fine art and commercial design — whether crafting a delicate brand identity for a florist or art-directing a full editorial spread, I bring warmth and intention to every project.
